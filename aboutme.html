Karis Kim

Biography

I am Korean-American, and often, because of my ethinicity, racial prejudices create disadvantages for me. As an Asian woman, many assume that I am passive and submissive, and because of that, I am constantly trying to prove myself.

I enjoy eating new things. In fact, I have even tried escargot!

I hope that I can be a pioneer and a role model for girls all over the world.
