About Me

I am Korean-American, and often, because of my ethinicity, racial prejudices create disadvantages for me. As an Asian woman, many assume that I am passive and submissive, and because of that, I am constantly trying to prove myself. This time spent trying to convince others that I am a candidate worthy of being recognized results in time wasted. Instead I could be making improvements and advancements to human rights and reshaping the way women are viewed.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.
